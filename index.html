Hello 😊 I'm Gabriela Vega, a Master's student in Media Informatics at Saarland University in Germany and a research assistant in the Sensorimotor Interaction Group at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, where I work with Paul Strohmeier on haptics and wearables.

Previously, I collaborated with Audrey Girouard and Karen Cochrane at the Creative Interactions Lab at Carleton University, and with Katia Vega at the Interactive Organisms Lab at UC Davis. I got my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences, where I worked on IoT and Edge AI for agriculture.

I am interest in wearables, haptics, IoT, human wellbeing, AI companions, and AI alignement. Feel free to reach out if you're interested in projects or collaborations!
